## What changed

- Mobile navigation was refined so the app feels steadier when moving between review surfaces.
- The first session received a calmer onboarding and auth pass.
- Review and Explore surfaces were tightened for a cleaner reading rhythm.
- Cover loading and analytics were tuned to keep the product feeling lighter.

## Why it matters

This release keeps the review loop closer to the center of the app. The interface should feel less like a dashboard and more like a quiet place to read, save, and publish music notes.
